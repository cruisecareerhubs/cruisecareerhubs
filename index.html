Basic Requirements

What you need to qualify for cruise ship employment

We're Here to Help You!

Don't worry if you don't meet all requirements yet! Many of our successful applicants started with limited experience. Our team can guide you through the process, help you understand what's needed, and connect you with resources to get the necessary documents and training. Your dream job at sea is closer than you think!

Age & Health

Minimum 21 years old with good physical and mental health. Must pass a medical examination.

Experience

Some relevant experience is helpful, but we can help match you with entry-level positions too!

Language Skills

Basic English communication skills. We can help improve your language skills if needed.

Documentation

Passport is required, but we can guide you through the application process. STCW certification can be obtained after hiring for some positions.

Background Check

Clean criminal record required for most positions.

Availability

Willing to commit to 6-9 month contracts and work long hours when required.

No Experience? No Problem!

Many cruise lines offer training programs for motivated individuals with the right attitude. If you're willing to learn and work hard, we can help you start your maritime career. Contact us to learn about entry-level opportunities and training options.
